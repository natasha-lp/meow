Cat Care Do's and Don'ts

DO DON'T
Feed a balanced diet. Don’t feed human food.
Provide fresh water. Don’t let them drink from unsanitary sources.
Keep the litter box clean. Don’t let it get dirty.
Schedule vet check-ups. Don’t skip appointments.
Offer scratching posts. Don’t declaw.
Use a variety of toys. Don’t use hazardous toys.
Provide a cozy resting place. Don’t force them into uncomfortable spots.
Brush regularly. Don’t neglect grooming.
Spend quality time with them. Don’t ignore their need for attention.
Keep harmful substances away. Don’t expose them to dangers.
Ensure they get exercise. Don’t let them become bored or inactive.
